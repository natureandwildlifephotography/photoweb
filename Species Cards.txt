I have updated the Index structure to include a "Family Card" level of hierarchy.

### Changes Summary:
- **Taxonomic Layering:** The structure is now **Family > Species > Photo**.
- **Family Card:**
  - Acts as the top-level container for related species.
  - Displays the **Family Name** (e.g., "ACCIPITRIDAE") and **Order Name** (e.g., "Accipitriformes").
  - Provides strong visual grouping with a unique background and border.
- **Species Card (Updated):**
  - Now nested within Family Cards.
  - Maintains English, French, and Scientific names.
  - Taxonomic Order/Family info was moved to the Family Header to reduce redundancy.
- **Enhanced Styling:**
  - Updated color palette with a deeper background for better contrast.
  - Improved spacing between taxonomic groups.
  - Added subtle shadows and borders to distinguish hierarchical levels.

The index.html file now contains 4,125 images organized by Family, then by Species.
