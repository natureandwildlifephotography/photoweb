### Changes Summary:
- **Taxonomic Layering:** The structure is now **Family > Species > Photo**.
- **Family Card:**
  - Acts as the top-level container for related species.
  - Displays the **Family Name** (e.g., "ACCIPITRIDAE") and **Order Name** (e.g., "Accipitriformes").
  - Provides strong visual grouping with a unique background and border.
- **Species Card (Updated):**
  - Now nested within Family Cards.
  - Maintains English, French, and Scientific names.
  - Redundant taxonomic info (Order/Family) was moved to the Family Header to keep the species cards clean.
- **Enhanced Styling:**
  - Implemented a darker background theme for better visual contrast.
  - Improved spacing between taxonomic groups for better readability.
  - Added subtle shadows and borders to distinguish each hierarchical level.

The index.html file now contains 4,125 images organized by Family and then by Species. Documentation has also been updated in Bird cards.txt and Species Cards.txt.
